Tuxedo Cats are Unique!

Here's why Tuxedo Cats are one of kind:

Tuxedo Cats are not a breed; they are actually bicolor cats. Bicolor cats only have two colors, black and white.

Tuxedo cats usually look very similar to each other. They usually have green eyes and white whiskers.

There are the same number of male and female tuxedo cats. Some breeds of cats have a higher percentage of one gender over the other, but tuxedo cats are pretty even.
